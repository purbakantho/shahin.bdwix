FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services, processes, and what sets us apart.

What kind of websites do you build?

I build all types of WordPress websites — from personal portfolios and blogs to business sites and eCommerce stores. Every project is fully custom and responsive.

Do you offer website maintenance after the project is complete?

Yes, I offer ongoing support and maintenance packages, including updates, backups, speed optimization, and security monitoring.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Absolutely. I can redesign your current site to make it more modern, mobile-friendly, faster, and aligned with your brand.

Will my website be mobile and SEO-friendly?

Yes. All websites I build are responsive (mobile-friendly) and optimized for speed, SEO, and user experience.

How long does it take to complete a project?

It depends on the project size, but most websites are completed within 7-10 days. I’ll give you a clear timeline before we start.

What graphic design services do you provide?

I design logos, business cards, social media posts, flyers, banners, and complete branding kits tailored to your business identity.

How many revisions do I get for a design?

I offer up to 2–3 free revisions based on your feedback. My goal is always to ensure you’re 100% satisfied with the final design.

Do you design for both print and digital platforms?

Yes. I create designs for both — whether you need high-resolution print files or optimized digital graphics for social media or websites.

How do you deliver the final design files?

Once the project is complete, I deliver all final files in your preferred formats (JPG, PNG, PDF, SVG, etc.), along with source files if needed.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! I’ve worked with clients from different countries and time zones. Communication is smooth, and all payments are securely handled online.
